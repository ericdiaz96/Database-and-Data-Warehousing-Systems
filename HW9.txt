Retrieve item number, primary description, secondary description and the price paid for the most expensive item ever sold. SELECT DISTINCT sc.item_nbr, id.primary_desc, id.secondary_desc, sc.unit_retail_amountFROM item_scan sc, item_desc idWHERE sc.item_nbr=id.item_nbr AND sc.unit_retail_amount = (SELECT MAX(unit_retail_amount) FROM item_scan)GROUP BY sc.item_nbr, id.primary_desc, id.secondary_desc, sc.unit_retail_amount;Item_Nbr	Primary_Desc	Secondary_Desc	Unit_Retail_Amount3553316	ENSEMBLE GRAND      	ELECTRIC PIANO      	6013.98QUERY 5) Query: Create your own original
